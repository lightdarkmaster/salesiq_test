Sales IQ
Overview

Zoho SalesIQ is a powerful customer engagement and live chat platform designed to help businesses connect with their website visitors in real time. It enables companies to monitor site traffic, initiate conversations, and provide instant support to prospects and customers. As part of the Zoho ecosystem, SalesIQ integrates seamlessly with other Zoho applications such as Zoho CRM, Zoho Desk, and Zoho Campaigns, making it a versatile tool for marketing, sales, and support teams.

Sales IQ
Core Functionality

The primary purpose of Zoho SalesIQ is to allow businesses to track and engage with website visitors. It provides features such as real-time visitor tracking, automated chatbots, and proactive chat triggers to reach potential customers at the right moment. By observing visitor behavior—like pages viewed, time spent on site, and geographic location—sales and support agents can prioritize high-value leads and offer timely assistance that increases conversions.
